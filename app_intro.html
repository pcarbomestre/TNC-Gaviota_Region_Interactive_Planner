Gaviota Coast Interactive Planner


helping users make informed decisions

The Gaviota Region Interactive Planner is designed to visualize and analyze spatial environmental data, with a focus on three primary axes: Natural Resources, Environmental Threats, and DEJ/EJ issues, with the purpose of helping users make informed decisions regarding land-use planning and natural resource conservation.

The app a part of a group project for the Master of Environmental Science and Management program at the Bren School, University of California Santa Barbara. The project aims to create a decision-making framework to assist Jack and Laura Dangermond Preserve, local agencies, stakeholders, and rightsholders in setting collaborative priorities and developing a region-wide conservation management plan.
